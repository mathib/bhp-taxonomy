building physical condition score (according Monumentenwacht)dp back to ToC or Data Property ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/con-tax/properties/bhp#mw-assessmentScore-BPC

Property to indicate the building physical condition (BPC) of an assessment (cp:Assessment) connected to an assessed object (cp:hasAssessment) according to the methodology of Monumentenwacht Vlaanderen. According to the methodology of Monumentenwacht, this type of assessment should be performed for each of the following six parts of an inspected construction: (1) the construction a whole, (2) the roof(s), (3) the rain water disposal system, (4) the roof structure(s), (5) the general load bearing structure and (6) the framework and glazing of doors and windows. The value of the property should be a positive integer between 1 and 6. The scale should be interpretted as follows: 1 = excellent 2 = good 3 = fair 4 = average 5 = bad 6 = very bad
