Abstract

The Built Heritage Properties (BHP) taxonomy contains OWL properties to describe built heritage aspects of constructions, construction components, spatial zones, damages, etc. The main taxonomy is structured using RDFS subsumption axioms, starting from either [contax:property_OP](https://w3id.org/con-tax#property_OP) or [contax:property_DP](https://w3id.org/con-tax#property_DP).

The namespace for BHP terms is https://w3id.org/con-tax/properties/bhp#

The suggested prefix for the BHP taxonomy is bhp

The Turtle version of the BHP taxonomy is available at https://w3id.org/con-tax/properties/bhp/bhp.ttl
The RDF/XML version of the BHP taxonomy is available at https://w3id.org/con-tax/properties/bhp/bhp.rdf
The JSON-LD version of the BHP taxonomy is available at https://w3id.org/con-tax/properties/bhp/bhp.jsonld
The N-Triples version of the BHP taxonomy is available at https://w3id.org/con-tax/properties/bhp/bhp.nt

The BHP taxonomy was created by Mathias Bonduel (KU Leuven) during his PhD research (2017-2020) funded by the Research Foundation Flanders in the form of a Strategic Basic Research grant (grant agreement 1S65917N).
